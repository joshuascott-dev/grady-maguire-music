About

Songs from the
Piney Woods

Grady Maguire is a singer-songwriter out of Gilmer, Texas — where the stories tend to stick to you.

Grady writes country music that doesn't dress up or apologize. It's the kind of songwriting that comes from actually living something — gritty, honest, and deeply rooted in the people and places he knows.

He's taken his songs to stages across Texas, Oklahoma, and Arizona, building a following the old-fashioned way: one show at a time, one song at a time, with nothing to hide behind but the truth of it.
